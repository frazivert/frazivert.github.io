About

I'm a junior data professional based in Olathe, Kansas, currently pursuing my Associate's degree while building real-world data systems on the side. My work sits at the intersection of data engineering, analytics, and machine learning infrastructure.

Outside of data, I run a Linux homelab, build PC hardware, and compete in Valorant at Radiant rank — which, if nothing else, proves I know how to perform under pressure and learn from every loss.
